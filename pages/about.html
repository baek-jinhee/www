Who I Am

안녕! My name is Jinhee Baek (they/them.) I've been creating digital art for over half my life. From illustration to UI/UX and graphic design, I enjoy experimenting with different mediums and growing my skills along the way.
My work is heavily inspired by the internet culture I've grown up with, pulling from years of online spaces, trends, and communities. A lot of my works are an amalgamation of those experiences, blending different aesthetics and pastel colors into something that feels like me. I hope to create things people can enjoy and connect with.
